Undergraduate, Computer Science & Engineering — Manipal Institute of Technology, Bengaluru (MIT-B). Expected graduation 2028.

I build end-to-end robotic and embedded systems — from multi-layer PCBs and low-power firmware up through mechanical fabrication, autonomy and field deployment. My interests sit at the intersection of robotics, embedded systems, autonomous platforms, and product design & manufacturing: turning abstract concepts into hardware that survives the real world.
