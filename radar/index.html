Flagship projects

Research & Development

Project Arachne

A bio-inspired hexapod robot designed for unstructured terrain navigation. Arachne serves as our primary research platform for inverse kinematics, gait analysis and reinforcement learning.

Adaptive Gait Control

Real-time terrain adaptation using IMU feedback.

Modular Payload System

Hot-swappable sensor modules for SLAM and environmental monitoring.

Project TrackMate

An autonomous athlete-tracking system for performance analytics. TrackMate bridges the gap between robotics and sports science, offering real-time velocity and posture data.

Computer Vision Pipeline

Low-latency player tracking using YOLOv8.

Autonomous Dolly

PID-controlled camera rig that follows the athlete.

Execution roadmap

Phase 1 — Procurement & Setup · Month 1

Establish the dedicated lab workspace and acquire core tooling, compute and fabrication infrastructure.

Phase 2 — Fabrication & Assembly · Month 2–3

Parallel development of the Arachne chassis, TrackMate vision system and BattleRobot armor.

Phase 3 — Testing & Iteration · Month 4–5

Field-testing Arachne on terrain, TrackMate with athletes and BattleRobot in combat simulation.
